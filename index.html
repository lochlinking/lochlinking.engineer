About

I am a mechanical design engineer. The thought of designing parts and systems for spacecraft fascinates me. Being on the shop floor with the machinists who breathe life into the very parts I design inspires me. Solving difficult problems with simplicity and elegance excites me. I have a curious mind and an eagerness to learn continually. My curiosity and enthusiasm have allowed me time and again to move a project forward when faced with challenging obstacles. I am not afraid of failure. I see it as an opportunity to expand my knowledge and make corrections so that I do not make the same mistakes twice. I see projects through from start to finish. I do so by striking a balance between time, cost, and perfection. I make cost effective design decisions that push the design as close to perfection as my timeline permits. With these qualities, I will pursue a career in aerospace and see to it that I have a hand in shaping the next generation of space exploration.
